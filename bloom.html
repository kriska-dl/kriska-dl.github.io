Bloom: Sustainably Fresh

Duration: 2 Weeks
Role: UX Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Testing and Visual Design
Tools: Miro, Sketch, Principle
Group: Dajella Overweg, Yanick Juin, Kriska de Lange

Project Overview

Bloom is a leading regional supermarket focused on offering high-quality products and a personalized shopping experience. They have an established network of premium brick & mortar grocery stores conveniently located to service their client base, which exceeds 30% market share in the region. Although Bloom has a long history of success with its stores, the management team is aware that more and more consumers are switching to online grocery shopping due to its flexibility and convenience.

The Challenge

Bloom’s vision is to design a user experience that goes above and beyond the e-commerce marketplace models that Amazon and other competitors (like Walmart) are offering. They want to add value for their customers by finding a way to leverage their successful premium store model and replicate it into a differentiating digital experience.

Our Process

We were only given 2 weeks to complete the research and the interface for this project. To resolve this time constraint, we worked together in an Agile approach. We also used the design thinking methodology to make sure that the final product meets our users' needs.
